Research Group

Machine Learning & AI Lab

Our research group focuses on advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence and machine learning. We work on developing novel algorithms, systems, and applications that address real-world challenges.

Current Research Areas

  • Deep Learning: Neural architecture search, interpretable deep learning, adversarial robustness
  • Machine Learning: Federated learning, transfer learning, few-shot learning
  • Computer Vision: Object detection, image segmentation, medical image analysis
  • Natural Language Processing: Language models, sentiment analysis, machine translation
  • AI Safety: Explainable AI, fairness in ML, privacy-preserving machine learning
